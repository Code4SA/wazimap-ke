{{ HURUMAP.name }} Our Land is a joint project of City Press, Rapport, Landbou Weekblad and Code for Africa. The data site gives infomediaries like journalists and civic activists an easy toolkit for finding and embedding interactive data visualizations into their storytelling on land ownership in South Africa. The data that informs the land ownership debate is often contested and incomplete, We will keep sourcing, cleaning and adding data sets to this site, and work towards becoming the authoritative data source on this issue

The project is built on Wazimap, an open source platform by OpenUp and Media Monitoring Africa for making census data more understandable.
